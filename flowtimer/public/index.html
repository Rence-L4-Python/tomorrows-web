WHAT IS FLOWTIMER?

A dashboard that leverages a productivity technique that adapts the rigid structure of the Pomodoro Technique to work with your natural focus levels.

SIMPLE FEATURES, COMPLEX TASKS

Set your timer, track your progress, and adjust your settings to match your rhythm. Simple tools that turn complex tasks into small, actionable goals.

Timer Selection

Use your favorite way of tracking. Choose Flowmodoro, Pomodoro, or Countdown.

Metrics

Track your hours worked over a period of time—daily, weekly, and monthly.

Settings

Want to change how your workspace looks? Choose from a selection of theme presets.

Perfect for developers, artists, and students who need to manage focus naturally.
